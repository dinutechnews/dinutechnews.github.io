## ğŸ¨ Visual Effects

### Animations You'll See

1. **Float-In Animation**
   - All elements slide up and fade in
   - Staggered timing for article cards
   - Smooth, professional entrance

2. **Hover Effects**
   - Cards lift up 8px on hover
   - Shadow becomes more prominent
   - Blue line slides in from left
   - Smooth 0.4s transition

3. **Background Clouds**
   - Subtle floating cloud shapes
   - Slow, gentle movement
   - Adds depth without distraction

4. **Counter Animation**
   - Numbers count up smoothly
   - 2-second animation
   - Professional feel

### Color Scheme in Action

**Background Gradient:**
- Top: Very light blue (#e0f7ff)
- Bottom: Almost white blue (#f0f9ff)
- Creates sky-like atmosphere

**Cards:**
- Pure white (#ffffff)
- Soft blue shadows
- Feels like clouds floating

**Text:**
- Headings: Deep blue (#1e3a5f)
- Body: Medium blue-gray (#5a7a9a)
- Excellent readability

**Accents:**
- Primary: Bright blue (#4a90e2)
- Hover: Darker blue (#357abd)
- Category tags: Light blue background
